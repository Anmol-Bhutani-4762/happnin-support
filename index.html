Getting Started & Account

To create an account: 1. Open the app and tap "Get Started" 2. Select your role — User (to browse and book events) or Organiser (to create and manage events) 3. Tap "Sign Up" 4. Enter your Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, and Password (minimum 6 characters) 5. Tap "Sign Up" to create your account You will be automatically signed in after creating your account.
To sign in: 1. Open the app 2. Select your role (User or Organiser) 3. Tap "Sign In" 4. Enter your email and password 5. Toggle "Remember Me" to save your login for next time 6. Tap "Sign In" Forgot your password? Tap "Forget Password?" to reset it via email OTP.
To reset your password: 1. From the Sign In screen, tap "Forget Password?" 2. Enter your registered email address 3. Tap "Continue" 4. Check your email for a 6-digit OTP code 5. Enter the OTP in the app 6. Create and confirm your new password 7. Tap "Reset Password" You can now sign in with your new password.
To edit your profile: 1. Go to the Profile tab 2. Tap "Edit Profile" 3. Update your profile photo, full name, email address, or phone number 4. Tap "Update Profile" to save Your changes will be reflected immediately across the app.
To change your password: 1. Go to Profile 2. Tap "Settings" 3. Tap "Change Password" 4. Enter your old password for verification 5. Enter your new password 6. Confirm the new password 7. Tap "Continue" Your password will be updated immediately.
To delete your account: 1. Go to the Profile tab 2. Scroll to the bottom 3. Tap "Delete Account" 4. Confirm the deletion Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data — including booked tickets, favorite events, profile information, and event history — will be permanently deleted.

Tap "Apply" to see filtered results 4. Tap "Reset" to clear all filters

Booking & Tickets

To book tickets: 1. Browse events on the Home screen 2. Tap on an event to view details 3. Tap the "Get Tickets" button 4. Select the number of tickets 5. Choose ticket type — Regular or VIP (if available) 6. Review the total price 7. Tap "Confirm Booking" Your tickets will appear in "Booked Events" under your Profile and you will receive a booking confirmation notification.
Your tickets are in "Events & Tickets": 1. Go to the Profile tab 2. Tap "Events & Tickets" 3. Switch between: • Upcoming — Future events you have booked • Past — Previous events you attended 4. Tap any event to view your full ticket details From the ticket detail screen, you can download or share your ticket as a PDF.
To download your ticket: 1. Go to "Events & Tickets" from your Profile 2. Tap on the booked event 3. Tap the "Download" button 4. The ticket will be generated as a professionally formatted PDF 5. Choose a location to save it in your iPhone Files app To share your ticket: 1. From the same ticket detail screen, tap the "Share" button 2. Share the PDF via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other app

Troubleshooting

Cannot Sign In

Double-check your email and password. If you have forgotten your password, tap "Forget Password?" on the Sign In screen to reset it via email OTP. Make sure you are selecting the correct role (User or Organiser).

Not Receiving OTP

Check your spam or junk folder. Make sure you entered the correct email address. Tap "Resend OTP" to request a new code. OTP codes expire after a few minutes, so use them promptly.

Event Not Showing

Check your active filters — date range, price range, or category filters may be hiding the event. Tap "Reset" on the filter screen to clear all filters and see all events.

Tickets Not Appearing

Go to Profile → Events & Tickets and check both the "Upcoming" and "Past" tabs. If the event date has passed, your ticket will be in the Past tab. Pull down to refresh the list.

PDF Ticket Not Downloading

Ensure you have enough storage on your device. When the file picker appears, choose a save location in the Files app. If sharing fails, try again — the PDF is generated fresh each time.

App Running Slowly

Close and reopen the app. Make sure you are running the latest version. Check your internet connection — HappnIn requires an active connection to load events and process bookings.
